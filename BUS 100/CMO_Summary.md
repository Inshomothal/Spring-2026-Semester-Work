# CMO (Chief Marketing Officer)

## Summary
Wheel Quick aims to solve LA’s parking and affordable transportation issues by offering quick access to cars for errands, commutes, and long-distance travel. The CMO is responsible for developing and executing marketing strategies to reach target customers and drive growth.

## Tasks
- Identify target customer segments and their needs
- Develop a marketing strategy (digital, local, partnerships)
- Plan promotional campaigns and pricing offers
- Research competitors and market trends
- Set marketing goals and KPIs

## Goals
- Increase brand awareness
- Acquire new customers
- Retain existing customers

## Timeline
- Q1: Market research and strategy development
- Q2: Launch campaigns and partnerships
- Q3: Evaluate and optimize marketing efforts

## Resources
- Marketing team
- Advertising budget
- Digital tools and platforms
