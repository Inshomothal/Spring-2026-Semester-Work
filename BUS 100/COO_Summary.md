# COO (Chief Operating Officer)

## Summary
Wheel Quick provides quick access to cars for LA residents. The COO oversees operations, logistics, and customer service to ensure smooth business functioning and scalability.

## Tasks
- Design operational workflows for car rentals and returns
- Set up logistics for vehicle maintenance and fleet management
- Establish customer support processes
- Plan for scaling operations as demand grows
- Monitor operational KPIs

## Goals
- Efficient fleet management
- High customer satisfaction
- Scalable operations

## Timeline
- Q1: Workflow and logistics setup
- Q2: Launch operations and support
- Q3: Scale and optimize

## Resources
- Operations team
- Fleet management tools
- Customer support systems
