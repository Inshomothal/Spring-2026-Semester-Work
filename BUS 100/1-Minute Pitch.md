# Wheel Quick Elevator Pitch

- Who's sick of trying to find parking on their own neighborhood street?
- Families, friends, and hermits across LA struggle to just find a spot to park their car so they can get home after a busy day at work or school. A big part of that is that you need a car to get you where you wanna go and you can't rely on family or roommates to chaperone you. You can't rely on Uber or Lyft to be affordable enough for something you may need every day.
- Wheel Quick gets you wheels real quick so you don't need to move from your sacred spot. We offer hatchbacks for grocery runs and other errands, sedans for daily commutes, and EVs for fuel-efficient long-distance travel if needed.
- We know money is tight so our prices are affordable with per hour rates and a monthly subscription that gives deals to the customer to help them get something good out of this.
- We're looking for investors to help scale with us on this venture.
